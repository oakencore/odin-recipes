Pancakes

Description

This is a recipe for thick, 'American Style' pancakes.

Recipe

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon of brown sugar
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 2 cups of milk

Steps

  1. Step 1. Beat eggs until fluffy; beat in sugar and salt. In a separate bowl, stir flour and baking powder together. Stir milk and flour mixture alternately into eggs, starting and ending with milk.
  2. Step 2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Do not turn pancake until tiny holes appear all over the uncooked side (top) of the pancake in the pan. Brown on both sides and serve hot.
